XHome Menue
Your servers & devices – one click away
XHome Menue is the MacOS menu bar app that lets you open HomeAssistant, routers, cameras, or any web interface directly from the status bar. One click on the icon – and your device is instantly accessible.
On the Mac App Store Screenshots

What makes XHome Menue special?

Direct Access

Open HomeAssistant, routers, cameras, or any web interface directly from the Mac menu bar.

Fast & Easy

One click on the status bar icon – your desired device opens instantly in the menu.

Secure & Local

Your data stays on your Mac. No cloud, no sharing – full control.

Fully Customizable

Add as many hosts/servers as you like and customize names & icons to your needs.
